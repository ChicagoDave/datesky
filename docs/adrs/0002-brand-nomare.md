# ADR 0002 — Brand Name: Nomare

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** 2026-05-07
**Session:** session-20260507-0258-main

## Context

The project shipped under the working title **DateSky**, a name derived from the AT Protocol / Bluesky platform lineage. Two pressures forced a rename:

1. **Platform independence.** DateSky's name implicitly tied the brand to atproto/Bluesky. The product may not stay on that platform; the brand should not constrain that decision.
2. **Product scope expansion.** Recent design sessions reframed the product as a **trifecta** — Dating + Friends + Community as peer first-class intentions, not a hierarchy with dating on top. "DateSky" reads as dating-only; it actively misframes the product.

Constraints surfaced during naming:

- **Trademark clearance must precede commitment.** Prior session attempted Pavilion, investigated pavilion.so as a domain candidate, then discovered an existing operating community platform (`joinpavilion.com`, 10K+ members, mobile app since 2019) with priority. That mistake is the primary input to this ADR's process discipline.
- **Inclusion-by-design.** Default brand visuals must not lead with queer-first identity defaults — the product affirms all identities but should welcome straight users without making them feel like a minority entrant.
- **Mineable metaphor pool is exhausted.** Five name finalists this session (Pavilion, Crossing, Loka, Stoa, Vellir) all blocked by prior claimants on the same metaphor. Names with transparent etymology in the gathering/community/threshold space are not clearable.

This ADR locks the brand and the chosen domain, and records the rejected alternatives and negative tests so future sessions do not re-litigate.

## Decision

### 1. Brand name: **Nomare**

- **Pronunciation:** *no-MAR-ay* (three syllables, stress on the middle, open-vowel ending). Anglicized form of the Italian.
- **Etymology:** "Nomare" is an archaic Italian verb meaning *to call by name* / *to give a name to*. The meaning is on-theme: the product is a place where people meet and address each other by name.
- **Word-formation provenance:** generated by a custom Python phonotactic generator (`scripts/name-gen.py`) restricted to a soft consonant palette (M, N, S, L, H, R, G, T, W) with vowel-ending CVCV-pattern syllables. The generator was run after metaphor-anchored name selection produced five blocked finalists.

### 2. Primary domain: **nomare.net**

- nomare.com is registered (since 2007, dormant Dutch parked page). Acquisition not pursued at registration time.
- nomare.net selected over alternatives (.app, .so, .io, .co) because:
  - The product's anti-precious positioning (bus-stop metaphor, local-tier proximity, anti-platform-launch tone) fits .net's open-internet, pre-platform-era connotation better than .app's Silicon Valley signal.
  - .net is a core gTLD with stable governance, not a newer TLD vulnerable to registry policy changes.
  - The pronunciation pairs cleanly: "no-MAR-ay dot net."
- nomare.net registered on GoDaddy on 2026-05-07.

### 3. Trademark plan

- File USPTO trademark application in **Class 9** (downloadable mobile application software) and **Class 45** (online social/dating services).
- Filing precedes any visual identity work or marketing launch.
- The class structure offers a defense against any phonetic-confusion challenge from B2B software brands (Class 42).

### 4. Negative tests accepted

The following phonetic readings were surfaced and accepted:

- *"no marry"* — read as on-brand (the trifecta product does not require any escalation toward marriage).
- *"no mare"* (no female horse) — inert, accepted.
- *"no more"* — fast-English collapse risk; mitigated by the chosen pronunciation (*no-MAR-ay* is structurally three syllables, cannot collapse to two).
- **NOMAR Garciaparra** (retired Red Sox shortstop) — niche association, accepted as inert.
- Anagrams (NORMA, ROMAN, MORAN) — all neutral.
- Japanese *nomareru* (passive of "to drink," meaning "to be swallowed/engulfed") — different word, suffix differs, accepted as a soft concern only.

### 5. Rejected alternatives (do not re-litigate)

- **Pavilion** — operating community platform `joinpavilion.com` since 2019, mobile app on App Store and Google Play. Likelihood of confusion in identical category.
- **Aviary** — cage/captivity metaphor; aviaries are bird cages. Wrong metaphor for an open-community product.
- **Crossing** — Animal Crossing (Nintendo, 47M+ unit franchise) cultural collision; railroad-crossing imagery. Marketing-fatal regardless of TM status.
- **Loka** — Loka Inc. (Silicon Valley software consultancy, AWS Innovation Partner of the Year) holds .com; Loka World (operating chat/dating app on Google Play); Loka Minecraft community.
- **Stoa** — Stoa Meditation (operating Stoic meditation app, paid subscription); withstoa.com (operating B2B SaaS); StoaUSA (Christian homeschool speech & debate); plus the dead-but-recently-operating Stoa MBA edtech.
- **Vellir** — Velir (one L) holds USPTO registration #77708578 in software services class (25-year-old digital agency, AARP/Harvard/Met Museum clients); Vellir Technologies (operating Shopify-software agency); plus the now-dead Project Vellir which had explicitly mined the Þingvellir → community-platform metaphor first.

### 6. Threefold (runner-up) — also rejected

- Descriptive but functional; carries no identity. Found in this session's batch but consciously deprioritized in favor of a coined word with positive hidden meaning.

## Consequences

This ADR constrains:

- **Codebase**: every "DateSky" reference (variable names, package metadata, About page text, OG tags, email-from addresses, README, comments) must be replaced with "Nomare." Phase work via session-planner; do not attempt as a single commit.
- **Visual identity**: future logo work proceeds under the Nomare name. Any logomark must support the chosen pronunciation cue (e.g., kerning that breaks `no | MAR | ay`, italic third syllable, or accent treatment that primes the Italian reading).
- **Onboarding copy**: must teach pronunciation early. Suggested approach — surface the Italian etymology as a small explainer ("Nomare — Italian, *to call by name*") so users encounter the right reading before they invent one.
- **Marketing tone**: the brand voice can lean into the "to call by name" frame — addressed-by-name is a thematically clean fit for dating + friends + community.
- **SEO**: nomare.com will continue to redirect curious users to a dormant Dutch parked page; this is a permanent drag until/unless acquired. A future session may consider an outreach offer to the registrant; until then, the .net is canonical.
- **Trademark filing**: any future ADR proposing a brand-adjacent product (e.g., a separate app under a related name) must respect the Class 9 + 45 filings made under this ADR.
- **Future naming sessions** (if any): must trademark-check a candidate **before** the user develops attachment, before any domain registration, and before any visual-identity work. The session-20260506 process failure (Pavilion: name selection and mockup work before trademark clearance) is the canonical example to avoid.

## Future Work Enabled

- **Codebase rename** — phased via session-planner.
- **ADR-0003+**: theming infrastructure, trifecta intentions schema, ZIP-based community-tier model. These are independently locked product decisions queued from prior session.
- **USPTO filing** — to be done as a discrete administrative task, not a coding session.
